## Sustainable Pace

:::info Work-Life Balance
Sustainable pace ensures long-term team productivity and well-being through balanced workload and regular rhythms.
:::

### Key Components

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Sustainable Pace] --> B[Regular Hours]
    A --> C[Balanced Workload]
    A --> D[Recovery Time]
    B --> E[Predictable Schedule]
    C --> F[Resource Planning]
    D --> G[Team Energy]
```

### Implementation Framework

<Tabs>
  <tabItem value="principles" label="Core Principles" default>

    - 40-hour weeks
    - Regular schedules
    - Planned breaks
    - No heroics

    - Reduced burnout
    - Consistent delivery
    - Team retention

## Best Practices

### Success Factors

1. **Team Cohesion**

   - Trust building
   - Shared goals
   - Clear communication
   - Collective ownership

2. **Continuous Improvement**

   - Regular retrospectives
   - Process adaptation
   - Skill development
   - Knowledge sharing

3. **Sustainable Development**
   - Balanced workload
   - Regular rhythm
   - Quality focus
   - Long-term thinking

## Best Practices

### Success Factors

1. **Strong Relationships**

   - Trust building
   - Regular communication
   - Shared understanding
   - Mutual respect

2. **Effective Communication**

   - Clear channels
   - Regular updates
   - Transparent decisions
   - Quick responses

3. **Value Focus**
   - Business alignment
   - Quick delivery
   - Measurable outcomes
   - Continuous improvement
